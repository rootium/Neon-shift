Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Neon Shift ("the Game", "we", "our") handles information when you use our mobile game application.

1. Information We Collect

Neon Shift itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from players. The Game stores only your high score locally on your device using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

2. Advertising

Neon Shift uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location data
  • App usage information

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

https://www.google.com/settings/ads

3. Device Sensors

If you choose to use the Gyroscope control option, the Game accesses your device's orientation sensor to control gameplay. This sensor data is used only locally for gameplay purposes and is never collected, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

4. Third Party Services

The Game uses the following third party service:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. Their privacy policy can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Children's Privacy

Neon Shift does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The Game does not target children and does not contain content directed specifically at children.

6. Data Security

Since Neon Shift does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal data at risk. Your high score is stored only on your own device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
